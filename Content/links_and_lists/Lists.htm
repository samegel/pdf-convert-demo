Simple Numbered List

Here is how we use numbered lists.

  1. Find a procedure that should be explained in parts instead of in a massive, difficult to read paragraph.
  2. In Flare, on the Home tab, click the Bullet List list and click Numbered List.
  3. Write the procedure clearly.

Simple Bulleted List

Here is how we use bulleted lists.

  • Find some related points that are not a sequential procedure.
  • In Flare, on the Home tab, click Bullet List.
  • Write the related points in the bulleted list clearly.
